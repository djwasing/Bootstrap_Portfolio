About Me

I grew up in Winona, Minnesota and graduated from the University of Kansas in 2012. I've served the citizens of Overland Park as a police officer for the last 5 years. During that time, I received several merit-based awards and commendations, including being named Officer of the Quarter in 2018.

In my spare time I enjoy working with my wife, Jackie, to remodel our 92-year-old fixer-upper home, riding my motorcycle, and spending time outdoors hunting, hiking, or walking our two dogs, Riften and Rosie.

As a Full-Stack Developer I have earned experience across the range of the most current and relevant coding languages for both front-end and back-end web development. In my current role as a police officer I enjoy working as a team to solve complex and dynamic problems, and I look forward to those same challenges as a web developer.

I believe technology has the power to make our lives better. I want to use my skills, knowledge, and creativity to make websites and applications more intuitive and efficient.

Please explore my website and view my Portfolio. You can also jump to my LinkedIn profile or Github via the icons. Enter your contact info and I will get back to you right away. Thank you for visiting.

~ D. Wasinger
